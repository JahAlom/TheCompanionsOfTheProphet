Family of Zaid

Zaid Ibn Al-Khattab was a sahaba. His father was Khattab ibn Nufayl and his mother was Hantama bint Hisham. His father was known for his intelligence in his tribe. Zaid had a sister who took the name of Fatimah bint al-Khattab. Zaid also had a brother who was also a sahaba and was one of the most powerful and influential Muslim rulers(caliphs) in history. The brother of Zaid possessed the name of Umar ibn al-Khattab. Zaid was also the older brother of Umar.
